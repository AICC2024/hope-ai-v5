HOPE.AI User Guide V1

Overview

HOPE.AI is an AI-powered education and compliance platform designed to help hospice care providers ask any question they have about HOPE (Hospice Outcomes & Patient Evaluation). Whether it’s about assessments, timelines, measures, or documentation, HOPE.AI delivers answers that are 100% based on official guidance from the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) — ensuring every response is accurate, source-referenced, and up to date.

Logging In

  • Visit https://hope-ai-4pa4.onrender.com
  • Enter your organization email and password
  • Don’t have an account? Click “Register here” under the login form

Free Trial & Licensing

  • New users can ask 3 free questions
  • After that, you’ll be prompted to upgrade your license
  • Licenses are activated per organization domain (e.g., @yourclinic.org)
  • Once a license is purchased, all users from the same domain get access

Purchasing a License

  • After your 3-question limit, you’ll see an upgrade prompt
  • Click Secure Checkout to begin payment
  • Select a license tier and (optionally) enter a promo code
  • After payment:
    • You’ll be redirected to a confirmation screen
    • Your license is instantly active
    • A receipt can be downloaded for your records

Asking Questions

  • Use natural language to ask about CMS regulations
  • Example: “When should the HOPE discharge assessment be completed?”
  • The AI will respond with a concise, accurate answer and a Reference Panel below the response

Reference Panel

  • Shows document title and page number
  • Links directly to the cited page in the PDF
  • Hover over a source to see the full filename

Viewing History

  • Click “View History” from the main chat screen
  • Previous sessions are saved and shown in reverse-chronological order
  • Note: Returning to the chat window clears your active session, but history remains available

Saving a Chat Session

  • Click “Save Chat” after a session
  • Downloads your chat log as a .txt file for saving or sharing
